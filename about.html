About Our Organization

Learn about our mission, history, and the people behind our humanitarian work

Our Mission

To prevent and alleviate human suffering in the face of emergencies by mobilizing the power of volunteers and the generosity of donors.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every community is resilient, prepared for disasters, and where vulnerable individuals receive the support they need to thrive.

Our History

1985

Foundation Established

Philippine Humanitarian Aid Society was founded in response to a series of devastating typhoons that highlighted the need for organized humanitarian response.

1991

National Expansion

Expanded operations to all major regions of the Philippines, establishing regional offices in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

2013

Super Typhoon Response

Led one of our largest disaster responses during Super Typhoon Yolanda, serving over 500,000 affected individuals.

2020-Present

Pandemic Response & Digital Transformation

Adapted services during the COVID-19 pandemic and launched digital platforms for volunteer engagement and remote assistance.

Our Core Values

Humanity

We prioritize human welfare above all else, striving to alleviate suffering wherever it may be found.

Impartiality

We provide assistance based on need alone, without discrimination based on nationality, race, religion, or political views.

Neutrality

We do not take sides in hostilities or engage in controversies of a political, racial, religious, or ideological nature.

Independence

We maintain autonomy from government and other entities while adhering to humanitarian principles.
